About Me

I'm a Postdoc at the Department of Energy's Argonne National Laboratory where I develop and maintain image reconstruction software for tomography beamlines. I like applying computer science to solve materials science problems, and I'm always down for board games.

I earned a Doctorate in Materials Science at Oregon State University in 2018 where my research focused on in situ mechanical studies of wood composites at the micron scale. I earned my Bachelors of Science in Materials Science and Engineering at Carnegie Mellon Unversity in 2013.
